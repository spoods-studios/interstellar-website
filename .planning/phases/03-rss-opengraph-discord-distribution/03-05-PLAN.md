<objective>
Make the whole phase permanently provable: one committed smoke script that a future change cannot
quietly break, covering the feed's structure, the metadata's coverage and correctness, the CTA's
presence, and the three loud-failure paths this phase introduced.

Every assertion here is offline and grep-based against real build output, in the idioms the repo
already uses. The two things that genuinely cannot be proven this way — that Discord actually
renders an embed, and that the W3C validator reports zero errors — are deliberately *not* faked
with a build-time proxy; they are human verifications in Plan 03-06, and this script must not
create the illusion that the phase can seal green without them.

Purpose: DIST-01, DIST-02 and DIST-03 gain a regression gate; the Wave 0 gaps listed in
`03-VALIDATION.md` close.
Output: `tests/distribution.smoke.sh`, auto-discovered by the existing runner.
</objective>
